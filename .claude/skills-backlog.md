# Skills Backlog

Workflows worth turning into skills once they recur. Promote one when you have done it twice.

## Seeded ideas (events company)

- **Project management sync** — connect to your PM tool (ClickUp / Asana / Notion / Monday) so the assistant can read tasks, status, and deadlines in real time.
- **Event research** — deep, context-aware research on artists, vendors, venues, suppliers, markets. (Example skill included in `.claude/skills/event-research/`.)
- **Run-sheet builder** — turn an event brief into a production schedule / run-sheet.
- **Sponsor and partner outreach** — draft outreach in your voice from a target list.
- **Post-event debrief** — turn notes or a transcript into a structured recap.
- **Ticket-sales pulse check** — compare sales against targets and flag risk.
- **Inbound triage** — sort supplier / artist / partner email into action buckets.

## How to promote a backlog item to a skill
In Cowork, describe the skill in plain language: "Build me a skill that does X. Here is when I would use it and the steps it should follow." Review what it proposes, then test it and refine.
